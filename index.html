about us

we take care of your healthy life

Boudy's Hospitals Group is the largest private health service provider in the Middle East and North Africa.

The group builds its hospitals (we have 1,000 construction workers ) and funds its projects with the support of the national government and development banks. The Boudy group operates its hospitals with the help of several German medical schools and schools.

Boudy's Hospitals Group creates a new branch each year, adding 500 employees per year, currently employing 3,000 people at a growth rate of 16% each year .
